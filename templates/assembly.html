Skate Assembly

If you are not provided with your own set of Marty skates, this page will provide instructions on how to 3D print and assemble your own. If required, please seek external support for this process.

3D printing

Access the file's for Marty's skates here. Extract the files. There are two provided: 'axles_and_wheels.stl' and 'skate_feet.stl'. Slice the files into gcode using your printer's relevant software. Print two copies of each file. Supports are recommended.

Assembly

Steps
  1. Slide the axles through the holes under the skates.
  2. Use a glue gun to secure the axle to the wheel. Ensure the wheels are not tightly attached to the skate otherwise they will be unable to turn.
  3. Leave the skates until the glue is fully dried.
  4. With a Phillip's screwdriver, unscrew Marty's feet, and reattach the skate in place. View step 10 on Robotical's build guide here for Marty V1, and steps 15-24 here for Marty V2.
