Welcome to Skating Marty!

What is Skating Marty?

Skating Marty is a lesson plan designed by a student at the University of Edinburgh for its Computing in the Classroom course.

What age is the lesson plan aimed at?

The lesson should suit children aged 9-11.

How long should I expect the lesson to last?

Dedicate around an hour to this project.

What will children learn?

Children will learn about product design and basic robotics and programming. This lesson plan is designed to inspire and excite children in early computing education. For more detail and source please visit the pedagogies page.
