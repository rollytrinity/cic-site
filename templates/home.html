Welcome to Skating Marty!

What is Skating Marty?

Skating Marty is a lesson plan designed by a student at the University of Edinburgh for its Computing in the Classroom course. This project is aimed to develop both design skills, computational understanding, and also inspire creativity.

What age is the lesson plan aimed at?

The lesson should suit children aged 7-9.

How long should I expect the lesson to last?

Dedicate around an hour to working through this project.

What will children learn?

Children will learn about product design, basic robotics and programming. This lesson plan is designed to inspire and excite children in early computing education. For more detail and source please visit the pedagogies page.

Where should I begin?

Access the For Teachers page to view the lesson plan and required resources.
