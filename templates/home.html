Welcome to Skating Marty!

What is Skating Marty?

Skating Marty is a lesson plan designed by a student at the University of Edinburgh for its Computing in the Classroom course. This project is aimed to develop computational thinking, programming skills, and increase interest in robotics and computing.

What age is the lesson plan aimed at?

The lesson should suit students aged 9-12.

How long should I expect the lesson to last?

Dedicate around an hour and a half to two hours to working through this project.

What will children learn?

Students will learn basic programming principles such as sequencing and loops. They will also learn the fundamentals of robotics which could leave room for further projects with other technologies. For more detail and sources please visit the pedagogies page.

Where should I begin?

Access the Lesson Plan page to view the lesson plan and required resources.
